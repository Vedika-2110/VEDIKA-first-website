Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of products does Calla offer?
At Calla, we specialize in a wide range of home decor items, including furniture, wall art, decorative accessories, and textiles. Our collection is designed to elevate your living space with style and elegance.

2. How can I place an order?
To place an order, simply click the "Shop Now" button on our Webpage. You’ll be directed to our online shopping form, where you can browse our collection, select your desired items, and fill out the necessary details to complete your order.

3. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. For your convenience, we also offer cash on delivery (COD) for select areas.

4. How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary based on your location and the availability of products. Generally, orders are processed within 2-3 business days, with delivery typically taking 5-7 business days after dispatch.

5. Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition.

6. Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we only ship within India. However, we are exploring options for international shipping in the future.

7. How can I contact customer service?
You can reach our customer service team via email at support@calla.in or by calling us at +91-98765-43210. We are here to assist you!

8. Do you have a physical store?
At this time, Calla operates solely online. However, we are working on opening a physical location soon, so stay tuned for updates!
